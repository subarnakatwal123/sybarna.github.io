The Journey

I am Subarna Katwal, and this is my story. A curious kid from Nepal who dreamed of creating a life filled with excitement, I embarked on a journey that took me from the classrooms of People's School (Nursery to UKG) to Green Field National School (Grades 1-5), Learning Realm International School (Grade 6), Deneb Academy (Grades 7-10), and finally Pentagon International College for my higher secondary studies.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor in Computer Applications (BCA) at Madan Bhandari Memorial College. But my passion doesn’t stop at academics—I live to create, break, and reinvent digital systems, always in pursuit of the thrill of the new and the unexplored.

Who Am I?

A self-proclaimed "Cyber Guy" from Nepal, I am on a mission to become the greatest ethical hacker of all time. With no fixed destination but countless ambitions, I navigate a life that’s anything but boring. Whether it’s diving into offensive security or mastering cloud systems, I believe in making every moment exciting.

My Vision

Life for me is about creating. It’s about doing something that stands out, breaking the monotony, and pushing boundaries. Whether you're here to know about me or to collaborate, one thing is certain—this space is where passion and purpose collide.
